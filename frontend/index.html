Our Mission: Empowering Every Nigerian Farmer

At Soilgenie, we believe in the power of innovation to transform agriculture. Our mission is to equip Nigerian farmers with accessible, cutting-edge technology that turns complex satellite data into simple, actionable insights. We are committed to fostering sustainable farming practices, boosting food security, and enhancing the livelihoods of farmers across the nation.

By providing precise recommendations on soil health, crop vitality, and weather trends, we help you make informed decisions that lead to higher yields, reduced costs, and a more resilient farm.

Bridging the Digital Divide: Empowering Every Farmer

We understand that not all farmers have easy access to digital tools or possess high literacy. That's why Soilgenie is built on a foundation of **inclusivity and direct support**. Our extensive network of dedicated agents plays a crucial role in bringing the power of AI to every farm.

  • **Personalized Assistance:** Agents work one-on-one with farmers, providing hands-on help.
  • **Facilitated Mapping:** They assist in mapping farmlands and inputting data, simplifying the onboarding process.
  • **Clear Communication:** Complex data is translated into simple, actionable advice, delivered verbally or via targeted SMS in local languages.
  • **Data Interpretation:** Agents help farmers understand soil analyses, crop health reports, and recommendations.

With Soilgenie, every farmer, regardless of their digital literacy, can harness advanced technology to thrive.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of data does Soilgenie collect from my farm?
Soilgenie primarily uses **satellite imagery** (like NDVI for crop health, and other bands for soil moisture) and integrates **hyper-local weather data** specific to your farm's mapped location. When available, it can also process data from manual soil tests you might conduct.
How accurate are the fertilizer recommendations?
Our fertilizer recommendations are generated by **advanced AI models** that analyze your specific farm's soil data, current crop health (from satellite imagery), and local weather conditions. While designed for high accuracy, we always recommend verifying with local agricultural experts.
Can I use Soilgenie if I have multiple farms?
Yes, our **Pro Plan** allows you to map and monitor up to 5 farms, and the **Enterprise Plan** offers unlimited farm management, tailored for larger operations and cooperatives.
Is my farm data secure with Soilgenie?
Absolutely. We prioritize the security and privacy of your data. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we adhere to strict data protection protocols. Your farm data is used solely to provide you with personalized insights and recommendations.
